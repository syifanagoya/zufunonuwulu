Andrew Luck is a former American football quarterback who is best known for his career in the National Football League (NFL) for 7 seasons with the Indianapolis Colts. During the course of his career, Andrew has won several tournaments and has been the recipient of numerous honors such as NFL‘s “Comeback Player of the Year”, AFC‘s “Offensive Player of the Month”, and NFL‘s “Passing touchdowns leader”.

Education

As he had to move to Germany due to his father’s job, he got his lower level education till the fourth grade at the Frankfurt International School. After his family moved to England, he went to The American School in London.

With his family back in the US, he attended Stratford High School in Houston and graduated in 2008. Then, he received an athletic scholarship from Stanford University from where he graduated in 2012 with a bachelor’s degree in architectural design.

    Personal Trainer

    Andrew Luck has gained quite a notoriety for most intense dumbbell warm-up routine possible. In this routine, he performs 6 exercises without any rest in between and each exercise set has eight reps. The warm-up exercises are as follows –

    • Hang Snatch
    • Squat Press
    • Bent-over Row
    • Bicep Curl to Shoulder Press
    • Upright Row
    • Lateral Raise

    This awesome and kickass warm-up routine is followed by a series of deadlifts, squats, barbell rows, jump rope and bench presses. He also places a strong emphasis on core work, which he feels is fundamental for a quarterback.

    Andrew Luck also follows a disciplined diet with a focus on eating lots of protein, vegetables and a decent helping of quality carbs. For breakfast, he eats eggs, oatmeal, yogurt, and potatoes. His remaining meals are comprised of rich protein sources such as fish, chicken, and steak. Also, the focus in on eating locally produced food items.

  • Andrew Luck loves traveling with his girlfriend and has traveled to India, Germany, and England among other locations.
  • The reason behind the origin of his famous lucky beard was that he hated the razor burn caused by putting the chin strap on.
  • He loves cycling and prefers to get around in Indianapolis using his mountain bike.
  • Andrew Luck isn’t a great fan of smartphones and uses a simple and old fashioned Samsung flip phone, which he had bought after his old phone broke down.

  • Due to spending some years of his childhood in England, he is an avid soccer fan and tries to squeeze in watching soccer matches in stadiums in his traveling schedule.
