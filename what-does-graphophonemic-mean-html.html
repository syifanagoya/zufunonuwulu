What exactly is phonemic awareness, and what does it imply?

When it comes to phonemic awareness, it is a subset of phonological awareness in which listeners are able to hear, identify, and manipulate phonemes, the smallest mental units of sound that aid in the differentiation of meaning units (morphemes). Phonemic awareness is the ability to distinguish between individual phonemes and manipulate them.

What is the smallest unit of sound that can be measured?
