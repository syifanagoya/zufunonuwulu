What is Chen Tianqiao's Net Worth?

Chen Tianqiao is a Chinese businessman who has a net worth of $1.2 billion. Chen Tianqiao acquired his riches as co-founder and leader of Shanda Interactive, a pioneer in China's online game industry. It was in 1999 that he teamed up with Chen Danian to form Shanda Interactive in Shanghai. Today, the company is said to be one of the largest entertainment and online gaming companies in China.

Chen Tianqiao decided to delist the company from the Nasdaq in 2011making it privately-held. However, the company has two affiliated companies that trade in the U.S., the Ku6 Media and Shanda Games. Born in 1923 in Xinchang County, Zhejiang, he is an alumnus of Fudan University and also plays the role of a husband and father of one.
