Dave Mustaine

David Scott Mustaine, better known by his stage name Dave Mustaine, is a guitarist, vocalist, songwriter, actor, and author from the United States. He is most known for being a co-founder, lead vocalist, guitarist, and major songwriter for Megadeth, an American heavy metal band. He is also the founding member of the American rock band Metallica. Megadeth has 15 studio albums under their belt and has sold over 38 million CDs as of early 2019. The band’s music and lyrics have sparked various debates. On Instagram, he has over 845k followers. Megadeth’s official Instagram account has more than 1.7 million followers.

Where was Dave Mustaine Born?

Dave Mustaine was born on September 13, 1961, in the United States. David Scott Mustaine is his given name. His birthplace is in the United States, in the city of La Mesa. He is an American citizen. He was born to John Mustaine, a father, and Emily Mustaine, a mother. His ancestors are French, German, Canadian, Irish, and Finnish. Virgo is his zodiac sign. He was a student at Marina High School.
