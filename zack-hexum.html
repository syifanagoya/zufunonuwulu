Biography

Zack Hexum was born in Wisconsin on April 28, 1978. Singer and songwriter who performed weekly at Hotel Café, Los Angeles and played saxophone for Michael Buble and Dredg. He released his debut album, Introducing Zack Hexum…, in 1998. He played keyboards, saxophone, guitar, and provided background vocals for Drake Bell’s 2007, 2008, and 2009 summer tours. He received his B.A. from the University of North Texas where he studied jazz. He played with the One O’Clock Lab Band while in school.
