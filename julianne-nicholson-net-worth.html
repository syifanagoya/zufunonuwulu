What is Julianne Nicholson's Net Worth?

Julianne Nicholson is an American actress who has a net worth of $3 million. Julianne Nicholson was born in Medford, Massachusetts in July 1971. In 2000 she starred as Marian Kitt on the television series The Others. Nicholson starred as Jenny Shaw on the TV series Ally McBeal from 2001 to 2002. In 2006 she starred as Christina Finn on the series Conviction. Julianne Nicholson starred as Megan Wheeler on the television series Law & Order: Criminal Intent from 2006 to 2009.

From 2011 to 2013 she starred as Esther Randolph on the TV series Boardwalk Empire and from 2013 to 2014 she starred as Dr. Lillian DePaul on the series Masters of Sex. Nicholson starred as Jean Jenson on the television series The Red Road from 2014 to 2015 and in 2016 she starred as Helen Torrance on the TV series Eyewitness. In 2017 she starred as Jill Lansing on the TV mini-series Law & Order True Crime. Julianne Nicholson has also starred in several films including Tully, Seeing Other People, August: Osage County, Black Mass, and more. In 2014 she was nominated for a Screen Actors Guild Award for August: Osage County. Nicholson married Jonathan Cake in 2004.
