Idris Elba Breaks &quot;Flying Mile&quot; Speed Record in Bentley: Details!

Another reason Idris Elba is oh so swoon-worthy! The British actor just broke the “flying mile” speed record while driving his Bentley in south Wales on Thursday, May 7.

“Flying mile” is a car’s speed between two points within one mile. Elba, 42, averaged 180.4 mph, beating out Sir Malcolm Campbell’s impressive 174.8 mph record from 1927. Campbell drove a Napier-Campbell Blue Bird race car at the time.

“I’m absolutely elated to have broken the flying mile at Pendine Sands,” the Avengers: Age of Ultron star said Thursday, via BBC.
