Now let’s dive into the meaning behind the song “Watchu Sayin” by YoungBoy Never Broke Again. As an avid fan of his music, I have always been drawn to the raw and authentic lyrics in his songs. “Watchu Sayin” is no exception.

In the first verse, YoungBoy speaks about the constant threats and danger he faces in his life. He mentions running in his stash and how things are bound to get wicked. It’s clear that he knows the risks he takes, but he refuses to be a victim. YoungBoy also mentions cutting off his house arrest bracelet, indicating a desire to break free from any restrictions or limitations placed on him.

Throughout the song, he expresses his loyalty to his city, not caring about what others are doing. YoungBoy refers to his haters and those who have criticized him, calling them out for their cowardice. He asserts that he lives life for redemption and will not back down from anyone. There is a sense of defiance and strength in his words.

In the second verse, YoungBoy dismisses those who claim to be tough or have achieved success through dishonest means. He questions their credibility and emphasizes his own accomplishments. YoungBoy’s confidence and self-assurance shine through as he asserts that he has built his success from the ground up, without any handouts.

The chorus of the song repeats the phrase “Watchu Sayin” as a rhetorical question, challenging his critics and doubters. YoungBoy makes it clear that their opinions hold no weight and will not affect his determination. He is focused on his own path and will not be swayed by negativity.

In the outro, YoungBoy continues to address his detractors, asserting his status as a thug and dismissing any claims that he has been taken advantage of. He confidently declares that nobody has ever taken a gun from him, asserting his resilience and strength.

Listening to “Watchu Sayin” resonates with me on a personal level. The song’s empowering lyrics and YoungBoy’s unapologetic demeanor remind me to stay true to myself and not let others define my worth. It serves as a reminder that success is earned through hard work and dedication, regardless of the obstacles we may face.

YoungBoy Never Broke Again’s music often reflects the realities of his life, making it relatable to many. His honesty and authenticity have garnered a strong and dedicated fanbase who appreciate his raw talent and ability to express his emotions through his music.

While the explicit content and aggressive tone may not appeal to everyone, “Watchu Sayin” showcases YoungBoy’s unique style and artistry. His ability to convey emotion and tell stories through his lyrics is what sets him apart from many other artists in the hip-hop genre.

In conclusion, “Watchu Sayin” by YoungBoy Never Broke Again is a powerful and impactful song that reflects his journey and resilience. It serves as a reminder to stay true to oneself and not let others define your worth. YoungBoy’s raw and authentic lyrics continue to resonate with fans, making him a force to be reckoned with in the music industry.
