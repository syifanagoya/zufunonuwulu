Sir Cliff Richard Net Worth

Sir Cliff Richard is a British singer who was born on October 14, 1940 in Lucknow, India. He has sold over 250 million records worldwide and is known for his singing style which is reminiscent of Little Richard. He is one of the most successful British singers of all time and has been a major influence on the music industry.
