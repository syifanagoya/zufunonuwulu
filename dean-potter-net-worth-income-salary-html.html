Denis Dean Potter (born January 18, 1972) in the United States is a free climber, alpinist, BASE jumper, BASEliner, and highliner who grew up in New Hampshire and now resides in Colorado. Hard first ascents, free solo ascents, speed ascents, and enchainments in Yosemite and Patagonia are among his many accomplishments.
